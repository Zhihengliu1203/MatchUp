Core value

MatchUp should match people, not just list events.

The homepage should immediately communicate that the platform helps users find the right people based on level, intention, atmosphere, and availability.

🎯

Better fit

Show level, activity style, and group vibe so users know whether an activity fits them before joining.

🕒

Easier coordination

Let people match around real schedules and real availability instead of wasting time with endless back-and-forth.

🤝

Real social compatibility

Match users with people who want the same kind of experience: casual, competitive, beginner-friendly, or purely social.
