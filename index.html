Homepage strategy

What changes compared with your current landing page

The goal is to keep the MatchUp identity while making the homepage feel more alive and more instantly understandable.

• More visual social proof: real covers and activity photos directly on the homepage.
• A clearer first action: discover events first, then join or upload.
• A better bridge to groups: event cards can later link to your existing groups logic.
